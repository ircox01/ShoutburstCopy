Customers to Rate Openreach Engineers in London and Liverpool

Customers of the BT subsidiary, OpenReach, will be able to rate the performance of visiting engineers in a trial in the South and North West of England. Presumably they will be rated on their ability to drill holes without leaving any dust, how dirty their van is (and does it have an obligatory comment made with a finger in the dirt, something like 'stop charging so much money for your telephone lines') and finally, how much 'bottom cleavage' the average engineer shows when looking down a hole and muttering 'is it the blue wire or the red one'.
